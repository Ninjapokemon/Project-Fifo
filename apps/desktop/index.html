Project Fifo

Draw on your PC. Stream to your Pi.

This editor sends full-frame updates over WebSocket to the Raspberry Pi controller that drives your MAX7219 chain with luma.

Status: Disconnected

Left click paints. Right click erases. Drag to draw continuously. The test-pattern buttons are handy during mapping bring-up, and connected Pi storage can keep a drawing library on the device.
